THE ARTISANAL PRIDE

01 THE ORIGIN

Located in the picturesque Yarra Valley of Victoria, Australia, Phoenix Pickups is a boutique guitar pickup manufacturing powerhouse dedicated to crafting exceptional gear for discerning musicians.

With a passion for the art of sound and a commitment to quality, we have been handcrafting our own pickups for three years, delivering outstanding tone and performance to guitarists worldwide.

HEART & SOUL

At Phoenix Pickups, we understand that the soul of any guitar lies in its pickups. That’s why we pour our expertise, creativity, and meticulous attention to detail into every unit we create.

We believe that each pickup has its own unique voice, capable of enhancing and transforming your playing experience. We meticulously select the finest quality parts from America, Europe, and Australia, ensuring our pickups deliver exceptional sound, reliability, and longevity.

03 THE BUILD

What sets Phoenix Pickups apart is our unwavering commitment to craftsmanship. Every pickup is meticulously handcrafted by our skilled artisan, who pours their passion and expertise into each step of the process.

From initial design to final testing, we take pride in creating pickups that not only look stunning but also produce breathtaking tone. By personally overseeing every aspect, we guarantee that each Phoenix Pickup is a masterpiece of sound engineering.

The results speak for themselves. Our pickups are renowned for their rich, dynamic tones, exceptional clarity, and unparalleled responsiveness. Whether you're a professional musician, an avid enthusiast, or a studio recording artist, Phoenix Pickups will elevate your playing to new heights.

YOUR VISION

We understand that your instrument is an extension of your artistic expression. That’s why we offer a personalized approach to customer service, taking the time to understand your unique requirements to recommend the ideal pickup that complements your musical vision.

Our commitment to customer satisfaction ensures that you’ll receive not only exceptional pickups but also a personalized experience that exceeds your expectations.
